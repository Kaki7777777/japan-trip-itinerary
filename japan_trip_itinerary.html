ğŸ“ Important Notes & Recommendations:

  • JR Pass: Consider a 7-day JR Pass for cost savings on long-distance travel

  • Azumino Highlights: Famous for wasabi farms, Daio Wasabi Farm, beautiful mountain views of Northern Alps, traditional rural atmosphere, local bakeries and countryside eateries

  • Festival Tickets: Confirm Asagiri Jam tickets and camping arrangements
  • Rural Experience: Azumino offers bicycle rentals for exploring the countryside, but with limited time, focus on local bakeries and eateries for authentic rural cuisine
  • Museum Timing: Japan Ukiyo-e Museum typically opens at 10:00 AM, plan Matsumoto morning accordingly
